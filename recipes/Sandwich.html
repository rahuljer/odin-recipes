Sandwich

Description :

sandwich, in its basic form, slices of meat, cheese, or other food placed between two slices of bread. Although this mode of consumption must be as old as meat and bread, the name was adopted only in the 18th century for John Montagu, 4th earl of Sandwich.

Ingredients

    Bread
    Cheese
    mayonnaise or mustard

Steps

    8 steps on how to make a sandwich
      Step 1 Get all the items out to make your sandwich.
      Step 2 You get 2 pieces of bread.
      Step 3 You put mayonnaise and mustard on it.
      Step 4 Put cheese on the bread
      Step 5 Put 2 or more pieces of meat on the sandwich.
      Step 6 Add lettuce and or tomatoes to your sandwich .
      Step 7 After you are done making the sandwich get you somethin cold to drink on.
      Step 8 Enjoy your sandwich!!
